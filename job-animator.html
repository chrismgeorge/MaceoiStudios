About the Role

We're seeking a passionate Animator to bring our characters to life with expressive, fluid motion. You'll be at the heart of our storytelling, translating emotion and narrative into movement that captivates audiences and makes our characters feel real and relatable.

At Sakura Dreams, we value animation that speaks to the soul‚Äîwhether it's a subtle gesture that reveals character depth or an explosive action sequence that gets hearts racing. If you love the craft of animation and want to work on original anime projects, we'd love to hear from you.

What You'll Do

  • Create key animation and in-betweens for character acting, action sequences, and effects
  • Interpret storyboards and bring scenes to life while maintaining character consistency and appeal
  • Collaborate with directors to achieve the desired performance and timing for each scene
  • Work with the cleanup and coloring team to ensure smooth handoffs
  • Participate in animation reviews and incorporate feedback constructively
  • Contribute ideas for character acting and scene staging

What We're Looking For

  • Strong portfolio showcasing character animation with a focus on acting and emotion
  • Solid understanding of animation principles: timing, spacing, weight, anticipation, follow-through, etc.
  • Ability to animate in a variety of styles while maintaining quality
  • Proficiency in animation software (Clip Studio Paint, TVPaint, Toon Boom, or similar)
  • Good drawing fundamentals, including anatomy, perspective, and gesture
  • Ability to work efficiently while meeting deadlines
  • Genuine passion for anime and character-driven storytelling

Nice to Have

  • Previous experience in anime production or studio environment
  • Experience with effects animation (fire, water, smoke, magic, etc.)
  • Familiarity with cut-out or hybrid animation techniques
  • Knowledge of Japanese animation terminology and workflows
  • Experience with action or fight choreography
