About the Role

We're looking for a talented Environment Artist to join our team and help create the breathtaking worlds that will become the backdrop for our animated stories. You'll be responsible for designing and painting backgrounds that range from peaceful rural landscapes to vibrant urban scenes, all while maintaining the distinctive aesthetic that defines Sakura Dreams productions.

This is an opportunity to shape the visual identity of original anime projects from the ground up. You'll work closely with our directors and other artists to ensure every scene feels alive and emotionally resonant.

What You'll Do

  • Design and paint backgrounds for animated productions, including establishing shots, interior scenes, and environmental storytelling elements
  • Develop location concepts and visual designs that support the narrative and mood of each project
  • Create color scripts and lighting guides to ensure visual consistency across scenes
  • Collaborate with the art director to establish and maintain the visual style of productions
  • Work with animators to ensure backgrounds complement character animation and staging
  • Contribute to world-building documentation and style guides

What We're Looking For

  • Strong portfolio demonstrating environment design and digital painting skills, preferably in an anime or illustration style
  • Solid understanding of perspective, composition, color theory, and lighting
  • Proficiency in digital painting software (Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, or similar)
  • Ability to adapt to different visual styles while maintaining quality and consistency
  • Strong communication skills and ability to incorporate feedback effectively
  • Passion for anime and animation as a storytelling medium

Nice to Have

  • Previous experience working on animated productions or games
  • Knowledge of 3D software for reference or base modeling
  • Experience with traditional media and plein air painting
  • Familiarity with Japanese animation production pipelines
