Background & Compositing Artist

The "World Builder" — You make the shot look like a movie.

Remote · Contract · Summer 2026

The Role

I'm looking for an environment and compositing artist who can paint lush, Ghibli-esque backgrounds and assemble the final shots. You'll be the last step in the pipeline, combining your backgrounds with the animator's character lines to create the final cinematic look.

What You'll Do

Background Art
Paint high-resolution, layered backgrounds based on rough layout sketches.
Compositing
Take the character animation and your backgrounds, and apply final lighting, color grading, and camera effects (blur, bloom, sun shafts) to unify the shot.
Visual Development
Assist in defining the color script for key scenes.

Who You Are

  • You have a strong sense of lighting, perspective, and color theory
  • You understand how to separate background layers for parallax/multi-plane camera effects
  • You're technically minded—you know how to make 2D drawings feel cinematic

Requirements

  • Portfolio showing painted environments and/or compositing breakdowns
  • Proficiency in whatever painting and compositing software you're most comfortable with

Bonus

  • Familiarity with 3D blocking to speed up background creation

How the Contract Works

  • Segment-Based Work — The story is broken into segments (scenes or sequences), each treated as its own mini-contract
  • Clear Scope Upfront — Before each segment, we agree on deliverables and compensation
  • Payment on Completion — You're paid upon delivery and approval of each segment

What You Get

  • Portfolio Rights — Everything you create is yours to showcase in your portfolio or reel
  • Published Work — The final animated short will be posted to YouTube, giving you a real, public credit
