The Role

We are seeking an Environment & Compositing Artist who can paint lush, "Ghibli-esque" backgrounds and assemble the final shots. You will be the last step in the pipeline, combining your backgrounds with the animator's character lines to create the final "look."

What You'll Do (The Deliverables)

  • Background Art Paint high-resolution, layered backgrounds (PSD) based on rough layout sketches.
  • Compositing Take the character animation and your backgrounds, and apply final lighting, color grading, and camera effects (blur, bloom, sun shafts) to unify the shot.
  • Visual Development Assist in defining the color script for key scenes.
