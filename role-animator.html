2D Animation Generalist

The "Actor" — You bring the characters to life.

Remote · Contract · Summer 2026

The Role

I'm looking for a character animator to take short stories and storyboards and turn them into final, hand-drawn character animation. You'll work directly with me to handle the full character pipeline: layout, roughs, and clean lines.

What You'll Do

You'll own the entire character animation process from start to finish. Given a storyboard and direction, you'll produce complete, polished animation—handling layout, keyframes, breakdowns, in-betweens, and final clean line art yourself. This isn't an assembly-line role; you're the animator, and each shot is yours to bring to life.

Who You Are

  • You have a reel showing strong character acting and weight (not just motion graphics)
  • You're self-driven and can meet remote deadlines without daily supervision

Requirements

  • Link to your reel or portfolio
  • Interest in anime or animation
  • Proficiency in whatever animation software you're most comfortable with

How the Contract Works

  • Segment-Based Work — The story is broken into segments (scenes or sequences), each treated as its own mini-contract
  • Clear Scope Upfront — Before each segment, we agree on deliverables and compensation
  • Payment on Completion — You're paid upon delivery and approval of each segment

What You Get

  • Portfolio Rights — Everything you create is yours to showcase in your portfolio or reel
  • Published Work — The final animated short will be posted to YouTube, giving you a real, public credit
