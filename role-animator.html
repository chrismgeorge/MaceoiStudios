2D Animation Generalist

The "Actor" — You bring the characters to life.

Remote · Contract · Summer 2026

The Role

I'm looking for a character animator to take short stories and storyboards and turn them into final, hand-drawn character animation. You'll work directly with me to handle the full character pipeline: layout, roughs, and clean lines.

What You'll Do

Key Animation
Define the motion, weight, and acting of the characters based on provided storyboards.
In-betweening
Smooth out your own keyframes or efficiently manage the motion to limit frame counts.
Clean-up
Deliver final, clean line art ready for coloring.

Who You Are

  • You have a reel showing strong character acting and weight (not just motion graphics)
  • You're self-driven and can meet remote deadlines without daily supervision

Requirements

  • Link to your reel or portfolio
  • Interest in anime or animation
  • Proficiency in whatever animation software you're most comfortable with

How the Contract Works

  • No Time Tracking — You're paid for the result, not the hours
  • Clear Quotes — Before starting a shot, we agree on a price based on complexity
  • Payment on Approval — Payment is released immediately upon approval of the final files
