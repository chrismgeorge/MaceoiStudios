The Role

We are looking for a Character Animator to take rough storyboards and turn them into final, hand-drawn character animation. You will work directly with the Director (who provides the story and character designs) to handle the full character pipeline: layout, roughs, and clean lines.

What You'll Do (The Deliverables)

  • Key Animation (Genga) Define the motion, weight, and acting of the characters based on provided storyboards.
  • In-betweening (Douga) Smooth out your own keyframes (or efficiently manage the motion to limit frame counts).
  • Clean-up Deliver final, clean line art ready for coloring.

Who You Are

  • You have a reel showing strong character acting and weight (not just motion graphics)
  • You are self-driven and can meet remote deadlines without daily supervision
  • You are comfortable working non-linearly (e.g., "I will finish Shot A by Friday" rather than "I will work 4 hours today")

Requirements

  • Link to Reel/Portfolio is required
  • Proficiency in Clip Studio Paint, TVPaint, or Toon Boom
