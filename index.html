Hi, I'm

Chris George

Making weird anime art, one dreamy moment at a time.

Background

I'm a software engineer at Google and a Carnegie Mellon alum, where I studied Intelligent Computer Systems for Visualizations. By day I write code; by night I'm chasing something a little more surreal.

Why Anime?

I've always been drawn to animated expressions—the way a single frame can hold so much emotion. I love the creation of fantastical worlds that feel both impossible and intimate. And I'm fascinated by complex stories that sit with you long after they end.

One day, I'd love to direct a show or a movie. But right now, I'm starting smaller: creating detailed, surreal short stories and moments. Dreamy little pieces that explore what animation can do.

What is Maceoi Studios?

It's not really a company—it's just me, and the artists I collaborate with. I write short stories and work with talented people to fully visualize them: backgrounds, animation, maybe even sound. The goal is to make something strange and beautiful together.

If that sounds interesting, I'm currently looking for collaborators for a Summer 2026 pilot project.
